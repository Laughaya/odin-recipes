Oreo Baked cheesecake

Description

New york style bbaked cheesecake with Oreo crust on the bottom and cream in filling.

Ingredients

  • Cream cheese
  • Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Sourcream
  • Heavy cream
  • Corn flour
  • Lemon juice
  • Vanilla extract
  • Oreo
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Prepare the cake tin with baking paper on bottom and side. Preheat the oven 180°C. Separate the cookie and cream
  2. Crush the Oreo bisquits and mix with melted butter and spread evenly on the bottom of the tin. Bake for 7 minutes. Set aside to cool
  3. In a large mixing bowl, Beat the cream cheese untill smooth and creamy. Add sugar and continue beating untill well mixed.
  4. Add beated eggs little by little and mixed well each time.
  5. Add sour cream and cream mix well.
  6. Pour a small amount of the filling to the oreo cream and beat well untill they are well mixed.
  7. Stir in corn flour, lemon juice and vanilla extract.
  8. Before pour into the cake tin with crust bottom, put the filling through shieve/strainer so the filling will be smooth with no lumps.
  9. Pour in the filling to the tin adn smooth the surface. Drop the tin from a fair hight to the bench top to remove big air bubbles in the filling a few times.
  10. Bake the cake with boiling water aside in the oven to steam bake for 45 minutes. Then reduce the temperature to 160°C and bake for another 60 minutes.
  11. Turn off the oven and leave the cake inside of the oven for at lease an hour. Transfer it to the cooling rack untill it completely cools down, then move it to the fridge and let it set for at least 3 hours before serving.
