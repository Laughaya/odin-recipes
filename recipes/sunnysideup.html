sunny side up

Description

Sunny side up is a one of the ways to prepare eggs which you do not flip.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Salt
  • Black pepper

Steps

  1. Heat some oil on pan with medium heat.
  2. Crack the eggs on the heated pan. cook for a few minutes and season them with salt and pepper.
  3. Cook a few more minutes. Transfer them to the plate. Wha la!
